Welcome to Abney

Nestled high above the Hope Valley in the Peak District National Park, Abney is a small, peaceful gritstone village with a history stretching back over a thousand years. Surrounded by open moorland, ancient footpaths and the wooded depths of Abney Clough, it remains one of Derbyshire's most unspoilt and beautiful communities.
